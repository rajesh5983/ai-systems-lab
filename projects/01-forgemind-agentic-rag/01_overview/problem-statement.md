# Problem Statement

## Background

Field technicians operating heavy machinery and industrial equipment often work in high-pressure environments where time, accuracy, and safety are critical.

In these scenarios, technicians rely on a combination of:
- equipment manuals
- fault code references
- maintenance checklists
- prior experience

While these resources are available, they are often fragmented across multiple documents and systems, making it difficult to quickly locate the right information when it is needed most.

---

## The Challenge

When a fault occurs, technicians must:

- identify relevant fault codes
- search through lengthy manuals
- interpret troubleshooting procedures
- ensure safety and compliance before taking action

This process is:

- time-consuming  
- cognitively demanding  
- dependent on experience level  
- prone to delays or inconsistencies  

In real-world conditions, this can impact:
- equipment downtime  
- operational efficiency  
- safety outcomes  

---

## Opportunity for AI

Recent advances in Generative AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) present an opportunity to rethink how technical knowledge is accessed and used.

Instead of searching across documents manually, an AI system can:

- retrieve relevant information instantly  
- synthesize it into structured guidance  
- adapt responses based on the query context  
- reinforce safety considerations  

---

## Problem Definition

How can we design an AI system that helps technicians:

- quickly retrieve relevant maintenance knowledge  
- translate that knowledge into actionable steps  
- ensure outputs are grounded and safety-aware  
- reduce reliance on manual document navigation  

---

## Project Objective

ForgeMind was developed as a prototype to explore this problem through a multi-agent AI approach.

The objective is to demonstrate how an agentic system can:

- combine retrieval, reasoning, and validation  
- support real-world maintenance scenarios  
- provide structured and usable outputs  
- serve as a foundation for workflow automation in future iterations  

---

## Scope of v1

ForgeMind v1 focuses on:

- knowledge retrieval from a curated set of technical documents  
- generation of troubleshooting and maintenance guidance  
- application of a validation layer for response quality and safety  

This version is intentionally scoped as a foundational prototype, with future versions expanding into workflow automation, system integration, and context-aware intelligence.
